********** Connect 4 Board Game *********
This is an interactive board game played between two players.
In this game, the player is presented with a 6 X 7 board and each
player takes his turn to mark one of the cells in the board with
histoken. The tokens are filled bottom up and the player who
manages to get 4 consective tokens  horizontally or vertically
or diagonally wins the game.

In this game, by default you have 1 dumb computer player and 1 human
player. However, users have the ability to select both players to be
humans or both players to be smart computer players or any combination
of players.

To begin the game please select the option number from the player list below:
1. Computer (Default)
2. Human
